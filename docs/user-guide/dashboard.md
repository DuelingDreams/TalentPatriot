## ‚ùì Common Questions

### Q: Can I customize which metrics appear on my dashboard?
**A:** Yes, use the customization panel to select relevant KPIs and arrange sections according to your workflow preferences.

### Q: How often does the dashboard data refresh?
**A:** The dashboard automatically refreshes every 30 seconds for real-time updates. You can also manually refresh at any time.

### Q: Can I export dashboard data for reports?
**A:** Yes, use the "Generate Report" quick action or visit the Analytics section for detailed export options.

### Q: What do the different alert colors mean?
**A:** Red indicates urgent items requiring immediate attention, orange shows recommendations, and blue represents informational updates.
