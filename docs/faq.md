## üöÄ Getting Started

### Q: How do I set up my organization in TalentPatriot?
**A:** After signing up, you'll go through a 5-step onboarding process:
1. Organization details (name, industry, size)
2. Team setup and roles
3. Primary hiring needs
4. Integration preferences
5. Welcome tour and first job creation

### Q: Can I invite team members during setup?
**A:** Yes! Go to Settings > Team Management at any time to invite colleagues. You can assign different roles (Admin, Recruiter, Hiring Manager, or Viewer) based on their responsibilities.

### Q: How long does it take to get started?
**A:** Most organizations are fully set up and posting their first job within 15-30 minutes. The complete team onboarding typically takes 1-2 hours.

## üíº Job Management

### Q: How do I create and publish a job posting?
**A:** 
1. Click "Post New Job" from the dashboard or Jobs page
2. Fill in job details (title, description, requirements)
3. Configure application settings and salary range
4. Choose "Save as Draft" or "Publish" immediately
5. Published jobs appear on your careers page automatically

### Q: Can I customize the application form for each job?
**A:** Yes, you can configure which fields are required vs optional for each job posting. The system supports basic contact info, work history, education, cover letters, and custom questions.

### Q: How do I create branded careers pages?
**A:** Go to Settings > Careers Page to customize your public job listings:
- Upload company logo
- Set brand colors and styling
- Add company description and culture info
- Configure contact information and social links

### Q: What's the difference between draft and published jobs?
**A:** 
- **Draft jobs** are only visible to your team for editing and collaboration
- **Published jobs** appear on your public careers page and accept applications
- You can move jobs between draft and published status at any time

## üë• Candidate Management

### Q: How does AI resume parsing work?
**A:** Our AI uses OpenAI GPT-4o to automatically extract information from uploaded resumes:
- Contact details (name, email, phone, location)
- Work experience with dates and descriptions
- Education history and qualifications
- Skills and technical competencies
- Professional summary

This reduces manual data entry by up to 75% and improves data accuracy.

### Q: Can I manually add candidates to the system?
**A:** Yes, you can manually create candidate profiles through the Candidates page. You can also import candidates in bulk via CSV upload.

### Q: How do I search for candidates effectively?
**A:** Use the advanced search and filtering options:
- Search by name, email, or skills
- Filter by experience level, location, or application date
- Use skill tags to find candidates with specific technical abilities
- Save frequent searches for quick access

### Q: What file formats are supported for resume uploads?
**A:** We support PDF, DOC, DOCX, and TXT formats. PDF files generally provide the best parsing results.

## üéØ Pipeline Management

### Q: Can I customize the pipeline stages?
**A:** Yes! Each organization can customize their pipeline stages:
- Add, remove, or rename stages to match your process
- Reorder stages by dragging and dropping
- Set custom colors for visual organization
- Configure automatic notifications for stage changes

### Q: How does the drag-and-drop pipeline work?
**A:** The visual pipeline lets you move candidates between stages by dragging their cards:
- Changes are saved automatically
- Team members see updates in real-time
- Stage history is tracked for each candidate
- Notes and feedback can be added during moves

### Q: Can multiple people work on the same pipeline?
**A:** Absolutely! TalentPatriot supports real-time collaboration:
- Multiple team members can view and edit pipelines simultaneously
- Changes sync instantly across all users
- Candidate notes support team discussions
- Role-based permissions control who can move candidates

### Q: What happens when I move a candidate to "Hired" or "Rejected"?
**A:** These are final stages that complete the application process:
- **Hired**: Candidate is marked as successfully placed
- **Rejected**: Application is closed but data is retained
- Both stages generate completion metrics for reporting
- Candidates can still be moved back to active stages if needed
